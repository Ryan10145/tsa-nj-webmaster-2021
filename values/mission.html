Our Mission

At the start of the COVID-19 pandemic, we recognized the need for remote medical services, and we established Soundwave to allow people to continue receiving medical assistance from afar during the pandemic. Our mission is to open up more opportunities for people to engage in virtual medical service, especially when they are unable to receive in-person medical aid.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Soundwave offer?

We connect you with the best medical professionals in the nation! You can check out the specific services we offer in the “Services” tab.

How do I use Soundwave?

Use the “Schedule Appointment” tab to book a date and time for an appointment. You can choose specific services and even request a specific doctor.

Where is Soundwave available?

We are available all around the world!

When is Soundwave available?

Our services are available to our clients 24/7, but our office is open to general questions from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM every day of the week.

Can I request a specific doctor?

If that doctor is available, then you can request a specific doctor.
